Hi, my name is Laurie and I have some advice for you!

Take your own notes!

Once you run through the exercises following the instructions, you've accomplished the goal but aren't always sure of why things happened. What commands made certain things happen and what dependancies there are between files that made it happen. Print it out and read through it. Look at each file and see how they are connected. GO back over the assignment and write down next to a command(class, id etc in CSS) why you put that in there. What were you trying to achieve and what was the result. Sometimes, when you sit down and really look at the details, and how they affect the big picture, it all comes together. There is an AH-HA moment where you say "That's why it worked that way!" and you will remember it better, not becasue you memorized the steps you followed in the exercise, but because you now understand what each step did and how it affected the big picture. Moving forward you can remember that if you do this,that will happen and be able to apply that one little detail in a completely different scenario and achieve the outcome you are looking for.
