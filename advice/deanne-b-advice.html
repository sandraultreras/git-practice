Hi, my name is Deanne and I have some advice for you!

Practice Regularly!

Consistent Practice: Coding is a skill that improves with regular practice. Set aside dedicated time each day or week to code.
Small Projects: Work on small projects to apply what you've learned. This could be as simple as a calculator or a to-do list app.

Be Patient and Persistent.

Growth Mindset: Understand that learning to code is a marathon, not a sprint. It takes time and effort, and it's okay to make mistakes.
Celebrate Progress: Celebrate small victories along the way to keep yourself motivated.

Have Fun!

Enjoy the Process: Find joy in solving problems and creating things with code. A positive attitude can make the learning process more enjoyable and sustainable.
Always remember, "Yes You Can!".
