Hi, my name is Ash and I have some advice for you!

Make a habit

Learning a whole new programming language is going to feel daunting. Practice helps! Set aside a little bit of time every day, and you will start to understand more and get better.

Ask questions!

Off the bat, you're not going to know or understand everything. Studying and practicing will help, but so will relying on your network of fellow Skillcrushers. Don't hesitate to reach out if you've exhausted your options!

Don't be afraid of failure!

Coding is hard. Sometimes, things won't go the way you expect them to, or you might not be able to implement a feature as expected. Don't let failure get you down - look at it as an opportunity for growth and get creative in figuring out a solution!
