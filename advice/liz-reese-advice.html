Hi, my name is Liz Reese and I have some advice for you!

Put it on your calender

Adding your lessons to your daily calender will help make it part of your daily workflow. Schedule each lesson, even if it's just a few a week or 30 minutes per day.

Treat Yo'Self!

These lessons are challenging! When you finish a lesson, 5 lessons, or an entire class, be sure to reward your accomplishments!
