Hi, my name is Jen Simon and I have some advice for you!

Not Everything is Black or White

When you first start programming, you are going to spend a lot of time in the "gray". The "gray" is a sea of ambiguity where you don't know what you don't know. Just getting started can seem to be a Herculean task. All you need to do is take a deep breath and take one baby step at a time.

Quality over Quantity

Remember that this journey is not a race. The only person you are accountable to is yourself. Take care to produce quality work over a subpar quantity of work.

Be Forgiving

Don't beat yourself up if you are struggling with a concept, or even the entire course. Walk away and take a break, try again, and ask for help when needed.
