Practice practice and practice!

When you start with HTML and CSS, it seems very easy in the beginning, but it keeps getting harder and more complex with time, at least for me. I learned that with each new project, I keep getting better and more conscious about what exactly am I doing.

I recommend to go through projects as many times as you need in order to understand everything and maybe to create your own little project. Maybe you have a friend which could use some cool website and like this you will help to both of you.
