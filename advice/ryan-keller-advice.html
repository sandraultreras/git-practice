On The Use of AI When Coding

If you use an artificial intelligence (AI) application, such as ChatGPT or Co-Pilot, to help you code, make sure you fully understand the code the application generates. This will make the collaboration between you and the AI more productive, as you'll have a better sense as to what prompts to give the AI to help you guide the AI in the direction of accomplishing your coding objectives.
