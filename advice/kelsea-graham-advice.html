Hi, my name is Kelsea and I have some advice for you!

Seek out multiple sources

If you don't understand the subject as presented by Skillcrush, do some googling. For example, I watched two different "Github for dummies" videos because the topic was just not sticking.

Bask in the objectivity

There's multiple, more effecient ways to do things, but it either works or it doesn't. I'm sure I'll still be told to make the logo bigger though.

Functions are fun

"If this, then this" seems to be the base formula in computer programming languages. So, maybe its less about the syntax itself and more about the efficiency/conciseness of your code.
