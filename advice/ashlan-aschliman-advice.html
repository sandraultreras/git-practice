Hi, my name is Ashlan and I have some advice for you!

You don't need to have everything figured out at the very beginning.

Give yourself some grace. If these skills are brand new to you like they are for me, it's going to take some time to retain the new information.

Be consistent. Sometimes it will be easier to go a day or two... or more without studying. Try to fight that urge and put in the hard work to be consistent. Even if consistency looks like 20-30 min one day and 1-2 hours the next, do what you can! Feeding your mind this new information everyday is going to help you remain at a steady pace versus a stop/start/re-learn path. Push, push, push!

Imposter syndrome is real.

Just like with any career or skill- everyone starts somewhere. You have to put in the work to further your knowledge to get where you want to be. Trust the process. You will get there.

Find community and reach out! You are not alone in your journey.
