Hi, my name is Brittney and I have some advice for you!

THE DOs:

If possible, work on your schooling everyday, even for just 15 minutes. Get into the habit!

Take notes, including detailed step-by-step instructions for later reference.

Google! Google! Google!

Work on side projects in order to retain learned skills. Make it fun! It'll come in handy later!


THE DON'Ts:

Don't be afraid to ask for help.

Don't be afraid to make mistakes. We learn when we make mistakes. So when we come across a similar problem in the future, we'll know what to do.

Don't be afraid to ask questions. Just think, children ask many questions. That's how they learn. Want to learn more about a new topic? Ask questions! People tend to respect others for wanting to learn more about a subject.

Just One More Thing...

I hope this helped out a bit! Learning web development is not easy, but it is worth the hard effort that we put in. Just take it a day at a time! On occasion, take a look at your progress to see how much you have learned!
