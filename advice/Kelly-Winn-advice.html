Life Advice

for newbie coders like myself

1. Don't Panic

New skills take time and not everything will click all at once. If something causes you to stumble, don't give up. Try again, research, watch videos, and ask questions. You already know more that you did what you started. Imagine what you'll know in six months, or a year - or five years!

So Remember: Don't panic, Breath and Keep Going.

2. Stay Positive and Be Curious

Try to be like Ted Lasso, my most favorite fictional hero.
Always find the best in yourself and others.

-yep, totally nuts!

3. Trust in the Universe

Speak what you want to have happen in your life and watch it manifest as you work and persist. Trust that you are exactly where you should be because skills are cumulative and you'll find your path - either in coding or where coding leads you.

4. Coffee + Hot Chocolate

If you haven't tried combining these two amazing drinks then it's time.

Directions:

  1. Fill a mug halfway with your milk or your nut juice of choice and heat.
  2. Fill the rest of the mug with hot coffee.
  3. Add a heaping teaspoon (or two!) of hot chocolate mix to your cup and stir.
