Hi, my name is Dandy and I have some advice for you!

Remember you're not alone.

Learning front-end development can be a rollercoaster, but your determination and perseverance are your strongest allies. As you navigate the realm of coding, remember that you're never alone.

Tap into the Skillcrush Slack community to connect with fellow learners and instructors. Don't hesitate to ask questions, share your struggles, and celebrate small wins together.

When you are stuck on a challenging concept, remember to breath, reference past lessons, check your notes, and that Google is your ally; countless resources and tutorials are there to guide you.

Honor yourself!

Learning to code might sometimes feel like deciphering a puzzle, but every "ah ha!" moment is a step forward.

Celebrate and reward yourself, especially when things don't make sense- you're learning a new language; how bada** is that?!

And when Git feels like you're lost in The Matrix, channel your inner TNT: Tank, Neo, Trinity (and Morpheus!); just like them, you'll work your way out of any coding labyrinth.
