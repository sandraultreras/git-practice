Know your resources!

When you start anything new, its always good to take stock of where to go for help should you get stuck, need to reference something, need a refresher on a concept, or just plain forgot the name of a command when working with GitHub. It happens to all of us! Whatever it is, it's nice to know what to do when you're in need of guidance.

Luckily, there is tons of web development resources availble. There's the Skillcrush community on Slack, lot's of great reference books, and Skillcrush hosts development Q&A's. Additionally Skillcrush provides downloadable documents for important terms as well as relevant sites to bookmark within lessons.
