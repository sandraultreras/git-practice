Hi, my name is Molly and I have some advice for you to use when learning how to code!

Trial by Error!

When you start coding, you may be learning something completely new! I found it helpful to take the focus off the pace I was completing the courses and focus on the fact that I was learning. If a problem comes up, think of it as an opportunity to learn something new and increase your knowledge, rather than hindering your progress.

Walking Away

When you have tried an exercise several times through and feel "stuck", it is helpful to step away from the problem. You can move on to something different or take a break. Sometimes this is exactly what I needed to find a solution.

Repeat!

Trying an exercise several times over helps me to figure out what I need to fix if the code is not working as expected.

Your Problem is Most Likely Not a New Problem

Befriend google, Skillcrush resourses, and new resources! If you have a problem, someone else has likely experienced the same problem. You just need to find it!
