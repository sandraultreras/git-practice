Hi, my name is Dawn Craven and I have some advice for you!

If at first you don't succeed, try, try again!

I know, it sounds really cliche. It honestly does work.

Learning new things you are always going to fail before you succeed.

Don't fight it, beat yourself up about it. Just accept it.

Failure is inevitable, no getting out of it, none of us are perfect.

Invest in yourself and your Future.

Check into as many resources as you can find and save them.

Look for books on everything learned to add to your resource library.

Even look at some of the middle-school and high-school STEM textbookson coding and web-design.

The more useful resources you find adds to the things to look for when stuck on anything.
