Know When to Step Away and Take a Break from Code Debugging

Debugging can be a frustrating processes. It can take hours to find a bug and you can easily enter a time warp. It can feel overwhelming and stressful, so you need to learn when to walk away. Go do something fun like a hobby. Its amazing how much this action can help you gain back clarity. Sometimes when you come back to it, the problem instantly pops out at you! Or, you will randomly think of the answer while doing something completely different.

Google is your best friend.

Googling things is not only part of a programmers job, sometimes it's more searching than programming! There is a plethora of information out there for the grabbing.

Words of Encouragement

Learn to tame the frustration on how long it is taking you to finish a project. The harder the game is, the more you learn. Plus, it feels really good once you've accomplished your project.
