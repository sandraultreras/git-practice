Hi, my name is Raanee and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

When you start programming your first web app you are doing something that you have never done before. In the case of software development it's probably never been done before.

Confusion, therefore, is a signal that you have engaged in the problem solving pursuit.

Suspended disbelief plays an important role.

When you are learning a new technology you don't understand HOW exactly it's happening.

This is a normal part of the process and in fact, it's a critical part.

learn to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand it.
