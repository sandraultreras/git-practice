Write or Type Out Notes for Yourself as You Go

As you work through the Skillcrush lessons, it is beneficial to take your own notes as you go because writing notes in your own words will help with retention and provide a better understanding of the concepts you are learning.

When you write out your own notes, you can write them out in a way that is considerate of future you after a significant amount of time has passed. As you go through the skillcrush lessons you will be learning a lot and may forget a lot as well, having notes to refer to that are detailed and all in one place are a lifesaver when you need to go back and review a step of a process or a concept that you may have forgotten about.

Try Not to Get Discouraged Easily

When learning something for the first time there will always be roadblocks and problems that arise because everything is new and unfamiliar to you. But don't give up! What you may not understand initially will come to you in time, and the more you sit with and practice the material, the better you will become! Also, if more information or direction is needed, there are tons of videos and resources online that can further assist you!

Don't Be Afraid to Ask Questions

Skillcrush mentors are available on Slack to help with any questions or issues you may have. Their responses are always thorough, helpful, and in a timely manner -- usually within 24 hours.
