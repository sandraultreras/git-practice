My Unsolicited Coding Advice For Newbies by Ruthie R.

1. Take A Break

Letting yourself rest is an essential part of the process of learning to code. If you're feeling burnt out/discouraged (or even if you're not), feel free to give yourself time to chill out a bit, then get back on the horse!

2. Build Stuff

The most efficient way to learn is through experience! Plan projects that interest you and that will be not too challenging but not too easy either and that will help you prove to yourself and your future employers that you know what you're talking about.

3. Believe You Are More Than Capable

It may be difficult to fight past the imposter syndrome and self-doubt, but it helps when you understand that the only one truly standing between you and your dreams is you! Believe that you can and you will!
