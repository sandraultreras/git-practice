Give Yourself Some Grace!

Have patience with yourself! Learning computer programming truly is a journey. You will experience frustration, confusion, and feeling lost during certain lessons. You will also feel extremely proud of yourself for deciding to learn this valuable and in-demand skillset that you can use to build cool projects not only while working for a company, but also while doing freelance work too, if you wish. Stay focused, stay consistent and keep pushing! You got this!
