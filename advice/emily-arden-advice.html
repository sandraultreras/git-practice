Well-met, friend! I am Emily, and here's my advice for fellow adventurers.

There are times where you will feel lost.

For those intrepid enough to journey towards self-improvement, it is inevitable that you will feel lost, or insufficent for the task ahead of you. It is only natural, for change is uncomfortable and adapting to a new skillset takes time and patience.

I have never seen your face, but I hope your heart is matched to mine. Ambitious, and determined. The path ahead has nothing you should fear. The only thing that could keep you from your prize is your own self-doubt. I know that not only are you capable, you are worthy and deserving of the change you seek.

Your path is your own.

Your trek is almost definitely different then mine. I want to assure you that moving at the pace that is right for you is not only permitted; it is required. I personally have had to take long rests and even stopped moving forward for a time. This is not a condemnation against me, nor would it be of you. You will reach your destination at the fated moment, and not one second before. I

I have not met another who has had the same strength as you. You are singular, and unique. And I am delighted and honored that you shared your time with me. Journey well, and keep kindness for yourself and others.
