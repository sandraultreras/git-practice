.....and I have some advice for you!

Just Start!

When you’re first considering learning how to code, or anything new for that matter, it can seem scary & overwhelming.🤯 Many times this fear can stop you before you even start. Be open & give yourself grace to LEARN. Tell that voice of doubt to zip it!🤐 You may not yet possess the knowledge to begin coding....but you will! Be courageous enough to take that first step.💪

In the beginning, watching video tutorials can be helpful but until you actually get hands-on & start doing some coding yourself, you’re not going to do any REAL learning. So go ahead...find that coding class or bootcamp, take a deep breath, jump in & JUST START!👩‍💻

Believe in Yourself & Don’t Give Up!

Ok, so you’ve taken the plunge & gotten started; congratulations!👏 Now you’re beginning to code, things are going swell & then all of a sudden,
💥BOOM💥, there’s something that you don’t understand that’s so far over your head you don’t even know the questions to ask to understand it.😧
This is when you’ve got to dig deep, believe in yourself & KNOW that you can find the answer somewhere.🔍 Ask your instructors, fellow students, Google, YouTube or any other resource you can find on the topic you’re struggling with.

Problem-solving will help you so much on your learning journey & will motivate you to keep going. I mean, do you ever feel as accomplished as when you figure something out that was truly challenging you? It’s an amazing feeling right?!🙌😁🥳💃

So, remember, you can do it! Believe in yourself & don’t give up; your future self will thank you!!
