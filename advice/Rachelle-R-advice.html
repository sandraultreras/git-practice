Hi, my name is Rachelle and I have some advice for you!

Small details matter...A LOT!

Pay cloase attention to the details. For instance, let's say you name your HTML selector skill list. Then you accidentally name your CSS selector skills list. This small mistake of pluralizing 'skill' will prevent the entire section you are trying to style from working. If you run into a problem where nothing you are doing in your CSS is changing any value, look to your selector names.

Don't hesitate to ask for help.

I learned my first piece of advice the hard way and didn't follow this piece of advice, to my own detriment.

I should have asked for help when I could not get a section of my code to work properly. Instead I spent countless frustrating hours checking and rechecking my code. I eventually figured it out but only after a lot of unnecessary self doubt and discouragement.

Don't let your fear of appearing foolish drive you down this dark road of self doubt. Don't let a stumbling block prevent you from achieving your dreams. Ask when you need help! This piece of advice is a reminder to me as well!
