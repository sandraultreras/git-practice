Hi, my name is Katie S and I have some advice for you!

Breath!

It may sound pretty basic, but on more than one occasion just stopping to take a breath has saved me hours of hair pulling, cursing, and tears. If something's gone wrong give yourself some grace. Go back a step or two. Remember that there is no coding situation that exists that can't be reworked.

When you feel like giving up...Give Up! (But only for a little while)

Remember that you don't have to learn all of this in one day. Remember you can't learn all of this in one day. Coding languages are big, complex things with a ton of nuance and sometimes counter intuitive logic. While some of the rules will start to seem like second nature, a lot of others may be forever a mystery. And that's okay!
