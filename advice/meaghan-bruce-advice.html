Hi, my name is Meaghan and I have some advice for you!

Take a break!

When you are working with logic and technical concepts, your brain gets tired.

If you try to force it to keep working without a break, the brain fog and frustration just gets worse.
Take a 10-15 minute break every hour and try to just clear your mind and not think. You will be surprised
how much more clearly you can work afterwards. Many times, the answer comes right to you after a break!
